Non-Ferrous Metals

Aluminium Scrap Supply & Processing

SchemaShine Metals is a leading aluminium scrap importer and exporter based in Hyderabad, India. We supply primary and secondary aluminium scrap across a wide range of industrial grades and forms including sheets, extrusions, turnings, and mixed alloys. Every shipment is graded and certified per the buyer’s chemical and mechanical requirements, ensuring high quality, consistency, and purity.

Our sourcing network spans across UK, Europe, and the Middle East, enabling a steady and reliable supply of premium aluminium scrap for foundries, automotive, construction, and extrusion industries. We maintain long-term partnerships with verified suppliers and follow strict inspection and documentation procedures.

Our Aluminium Scrap Range Includes:

  • Taint/Tabor: Clean mixed old alloy sheet scrap, ideal for general remelting and foundry applications.
  • Tread & Cast Alloys: Foundry-grade scrap suitable for die casting and alloying.
  • UBC (Used Beverage Cans): 100% recyclable material used in extrusion-grade billets and ingots.
  • Extrusions (6063, 6061): Clean aluminium profiles for billet casting and automotive use.
  • Turnings & Borings: Segregated, oil-free materials ideal for secondary melting operations.
  • Mixed Aluminium Grades: Custom-processed and sorted per buyer’s composition and melt requirement.

Quality Control & Documentation:

  • Each shipment undergoes thorough inspection, cleaning, and grading by trained professionals.
  • Chemical Analysis Reports and Weight Tickets are provided with every consignment.
  • Customized packaging options: compressed bales, loose loading, or containerized shipments.
  • All trade documentation prepared: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading (B/L), Certificate of Origin, and Inspection Reports when required.
  • Flexible Incoterms (FOB / CFR / CIF) to suit buyer preferences.

Applications:

  • Automotive and aerospace component manufacturing
  • Construction, extrusion, and building materials industries
  • Electrical conductor and cable production
  • Foundry and die casting alloy manufacturing

As an ISO-compliant organisation, SchemaShine Metals focuses on ethical sourcing, environmental responsibility, and sustainable recycling of non-ferrous metals. We aim to provide cost-effective, high-quality aluminium scrap solutions for clients across India, Europe, and Asia.

Brass & Copper

Brass pieces, turnings and copper scrap available with grading and testing. We can provide sampling & lab reports upon request for large orders.

Packing & Shipment

Non-ferrous items are packed safely to prevent contamination and shipped in containers or trailers. Details and photos provided pre-shipment.
