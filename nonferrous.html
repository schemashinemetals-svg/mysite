Our Non-Ferrous Materials

SchemaShine Metals is a trusted supplier and exporter of aluminium scrap with reliable monthly loading capacity and strong logistics support. We export high-quality aluminium scrap to foundries, recycling plants, and smelters across India, UK, UAE, Vietnam, and other global markets.

  • Aluminium Scrap (All Grades) — From extrusion and casting to sheet and mixed grades.
  • Brass Scrap — High-quality yellow brass and mixed brass solids suitable for re-melting.
  • Copper Scrap — Including Berry, Candy, and Millberry grades with verified purity.
  • Mixed Non-Ferrous Materials — Custom-graded lots based on buyer specifications.
