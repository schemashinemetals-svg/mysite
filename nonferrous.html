Aluminium Scrap Supplier & Non-Ferrous Metal Exporter

SchemaShine Metals is a UK-based aluminium scrap supplier and non-ferrous metal exporter, specializing in premium-grade aluminium scrap, brass scrap, and copper scrap. We supply recycling plants, smelters, and foundries worldwide with consistent quality, transparent grading, and export-ready materials that meet international recycling standards.


Aluminium Scrap Grades We Supply

Our aluminium scrap is carefully sourced, processed, and graded in the UK to meet buyer-specific requirements. We supply all major ISRI-recognized aluminium scrap grades suitable for melting, recycling, and secondary aluminium production.

  • Tense Aluminium Scrap – Clean cast aluminium, free from excessive contamination
  • Taint / Tabor – Mixed aluminium scrap including old sheet and extrusion
  • Zorba – Shredded mixed non-ferrous scrap with aluminium content
  • Aluminium UBC Scrap – Used beverage cans, baled or loose
  • Aluminium Wheel Scrap – Alloy wheels, painted or unpainted
  • Aluminium Extrusion Scrap – 6063, 6061, and mixed extrusion grades
  • Aluminium Tread & Sheet Scrap – Clean and mixed aluminium sheets
  • Telic & Talk – Transmission and mixed aluminium scrap grades

Aluminium Scrap & Non-Ferrous Metals We Export

SchemaShine Metals is a reliable aluminium scrap exporter from the UK, supplying industrial-grade non-ferrous metals to global recycling markets. Our materials are shipped with full documentation and quality assurance.
