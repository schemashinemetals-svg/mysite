Ferrous Scrap — HMS 1 & HMS 2

Products

SchemaShine Metals is a trusted UK-based supplier and exporter of HMS 1 & HMS 2 ferrous scrap, sourced from reliable and certified yards across the United Kingdom. Our materials are carefully graded, processed, and loaded under supervision to ensure compliance with ISRI standards and international buyer specifications.

We specialize in supplying Heavy Melting Scrap (HMS) to steel mills, foundries, and re-rolling plants worldwide. Our ferrous scrap is magnetically sorted, cut to size, and free from impurities like non-ferrous metals, dirt, or excessive rust. Every shipment is documented, inspected, and weighed before dispatch.

HMS 1 (ISRI 200–202)

  • Comprises heavier, clean steel scrap — such as beams, plates, rebar, and structural sections.
  • Contains no galvanized or blackened steel — ensuring high melting yield and low contamination.
  • Preferred for steel re-rolling and billet production due to consistent density and quality.
  • Cut to standard sizes suitable for containerized or bulk vessel loading.

HMS 2 (ISRI 203–206)

  • Consists of lighter mixed scrap including blackened, galvanized, or thinner steel sheets.
  • Ideal for melting and reprocessing in furnaces requiring variable composition scrap.
  • Includes pipes, plates, and shredded steel that meet international mill requirements.
  • Processed and loaded under strict quality and weight verification protocols.

Quality & Loading

Each consignment is inspected. Buyers may request pre-shipment photos, weighbridge tickets and third-party inspection certificates. We offer supervised loading to minimise claims on arrival.

Typical Volume & Packaging

Shipments range from LCL to full containers (FCL). For bulk shipments we arrange container stuffing or direct vessel loads where applicable, with standard commercial invoices, packing lists and bills of lading provided.

Ports of Discharge

We coordinate discharges primarily at Chennai, Nhava Sheva (JNPT), Mundra and Goa with experienced local agents.
