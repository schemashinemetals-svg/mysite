Our Ferrous Scrap Services

SchemaShine Metals specializes in HMS 1 & 2 ferrous scrap, sourced from reliable UK and global suppliers. Our material is sorted, graded, and inspected to meet client specifications, ensuring top-quality delivery to Chennai, Nhava Sheva, Mundra, Goa, and worldwide.

HMS 1

Heavier, cleaner scrap, primarily structural and industrial sources — ideal for re-rolling and steel production.

HMS 2

Mixed scrap including lighter and shredded materials, suitable for melting and secondary steel production.

Quality Assurance

All shipments are inspected and graded at source with supervised loading at ports, ensuring client satisfaction and compliance.
