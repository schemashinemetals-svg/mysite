About SchemaShine Metals

About SchemaShine Metals

SchemaShine Metals is a trusted and professionally managed aluminium scrap importer and exporter with over 15 years of global trading experience. Established in the United Kingdom, SchemaShine Metals has built a strong international network of suppliers and buyers across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Our operations are centered on transparency, quality assurance, and long-term business partnerships.

Our founders, educated and trained in the UK metal recycling industry, have expanded our export operations primarily to India through major ports including Chennai, Nhava Sheva (JNPT, Mumbai), Mundra, and Goa. This global foundation enables us to offer competitive pricing, consistent supply, and certified quality to foundries, alloy manufacturers, and industrial consumers.

We supply primary and secondary aluminium scrap across a wide range of industrial grades and forms including sheets, extrusions, turnings, and mixed alloys. Each consignment is thoroughly inspected, processed, and certified according to buyer specifications to ensure purity and performance.

Our Mission

To connect reliable UK sourcing with responsible buyers worldwide by ensuring traceable, compliant, and well-graded aluminium scrap shipments that meet and exceed buyer specifications.

We continuously strive to promote sustainable recycling practices and enhance global trade efficiency by building trusted partnerships between suppliers and buyers in the aluminium scrap market.

What we export & manage

  • HMS 1 & HMS 2 (ferrous steel scrap)
  • Aluminium scrap (all common alloys & forms)
  • Brass, Copper & other non-ferrous materials
  • Custom blends as per mill requirements

Quality & Compliance

All loads are checked and supervised prior to shipment. Documentation and certificates (where required) are provided — quality inspections, photos, and weighbridge tickets are standard for shipments to India and other markets.

Our Ports & Partners

Primary ports of discharge: Chennai Port, Nhava Sheva (JNPT), Mundra, Goa. We coordinate with local agents for smooth customs clearance and logistics on arrival.
