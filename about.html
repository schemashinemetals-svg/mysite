About SchemaShine Metals

UK & Global exporter of HMS 1 & 2, aluminium, brass, copper, and other non-ferrous scrap. Reliable sourcing and shipping worldwide for over 15 years.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to connect reliable UK and global scrap suppliers to international buyers, SchemaShine Metals has grown into a trusted exporter of ferrous and non-ferrous metals. Our expertise covers HMS 1 & 2, aluminium of all grades, brass, copper, and mixed non-ferrous scrap, with a focus on compliance, quality, and timely delivery.


Our Mission

To provide seamless sourcing and export solutions for HMS and non-ferrous scrap globally while ensuring transparent, buyer-friendly operations.


Our Strengths

  • 15+ years of industry experience
  • Trusted UK & global supplier network
  • Full documentation & QA support
  • Supervised loading & competitive pricing

Global Reach

Exports primarily to India (Chennai, Nhava Sheva, Mundra, Goa) and other international ports. We maintain strict compliance and grading at source to meet buyers' specifications worldwide.
